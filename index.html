CARE AND HEALT FOR
YOUR CACTUS

  • In cold times, add water once a month an during the summer do it when the soil is very dry.

  • Have good drainage so that the cactus does not accumulate water.

  • Place your cactus or succulent in a location with indirect light.

  • Do not water more than necessary, as it can accumulate to much water and the plant will rot.

  • Do not expose to high temperatures, as succulent plants are not fans os extremes.
